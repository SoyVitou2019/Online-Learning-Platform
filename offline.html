Work hard, play Game

"Work hard, play Game" is a phrase that encapsulates a balanced approach to life, emphasizing the importance of both diligence in one's professional or personal pursuits and the need for relaxation, leisure, and enjoyment. This mantra suggests that individuals should dedicate themselves to their work or responsibilities with determination and commitment, putting in the effort required to achieve their goals or fulfill their duties.
